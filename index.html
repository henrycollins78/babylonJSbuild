The game is available on various platforms, including Microsoft Windows, macOS, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S, Nintendo Switch, and Android. The game has two main modes: Save the World and Battle Royale. In Save the World mode, players cooperate to defend themselves against zombie-like creatures. In Battle Royale mode, up to 100 players fight to be the last person standing on an island. Players can collect resources and build structures to protect themselves from enemy fire.
